How to play this game?

  • By rolling the dice , the current player's score will increase by the rolled value
  • Avoid rolling a 1, as it will reset the player's current score to 0, and the turn will switch to the other player.
  • Click "Hold" to add the player's current score to their total score. The first player to reach or exceed a total score of 100 wins the game.
